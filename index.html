About

I'm a graduate student at the cand.merc.it - data science programme at Copenhagen Business School. The programme combines hands-on data science with statistical and mathematical theory. I work as a graduate research assistant within the field of Natural Language Processing in the Department of Management, Society and Communication @ CBS. My focus in general is simply on becoming a (better) data scientist. There's several aspects of data science and I'm trying to get familiar with all of them, little by little.

    MSc. in Data Science

    The cand.merc.it - Data Science programme at Copenhagen Business School uniquely combines hard analytical skills with an understanding of the relevant business data context for application. Through this combination students learn how to use theories, models and tools for data analytics to generate actionable insights and develop fact-based platforms for decision making by conducting visual, text and predictive analysis.


    The courses cover a wide range of subjects, with a mixture of solid mathematical and statistical theory and hands-on development and analysis using R and python. See link for full course overview.

    2016 - 2019

    BSc. in Business Administration & IT

    The HA(it.) programme at Copenhagen Business School provides graduates with a broad understanding of the opportunities and challenges that companies face in relation to IT. Students gain competencies to develop, implement and evaluate IT systems while factoring in business-side considerations, financials and organizational factors.


    The programme includes economic courses such as finance, macroeconomics, and financial accounting, as well as hands-on development in Java and JavaScript. distributed systems, development of larger systems, IT project management and IT change management.
